Hi! We're WITS.

WITS is a society for UOW students interested in IT. Members can socialise, work together on projects and gain exposure in the industry. We have members interested in programming, networking, gaming - really, anything related to computers!

WITS run a number of events throughout session to benefit members. Past events have included Drive With WITS, High iTea, Linux Installfest and Hackagong.
