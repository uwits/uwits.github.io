WITS is the Wollongong Information Technology Society at the University of Wollongong.

Hi! We are WITS, the University of Wollongong Information Technology Society (almost as bad as S.H.I.E.L.D). We are a society for UOW students interested in technology and the IT community. Members can socialise, work together on projects, learn about technology and gain exposure in the industry. We have members interested in programming, networking, gaming - really, anything related to computers!

WITS run a number of events throughout session to benefit members. Past events have included Drive With WITS, High iTea, Linux Installfest and Hackagong. Our aim is to ensure that the IT community at UOW and the Illawarra prosper, with those involved gaining invaluable experience and knowledge for the future.

We like to mix things up a little bit, ensuring that there are a good mixture of fun and entertaining events as well as industry and experience based workshops and events. WITS has some cool tech that we love to show to those around campus.
